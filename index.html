About Me

I am a researcher with expertise in computer science and bioinformatics, including a PhD from the School of Computing and Information Systems at the University of Melbourne. My work focuses on developing and applying computational and statistical approaches to complex challenges in the life sciences, particularly in medical and biological data analysis. I also have extensive experience in software engineering and high-performance computing, enabling the design of scalable, efficient solutions for scientific applications.
